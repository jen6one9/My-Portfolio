About Me

Jeneth Diesta is an IT project manager for a global financial institution with experience in managing offshore/onshore teams for multi-million dollar technology projects. Jeneth began her journey in tech as a systems analyst, being exposed to the backend mainframe systems that feed into online applications. She is a Certified Scrum Master and is looking to expand her skillset by becoming an IT Tech Lead.

Jeneth believes in the power of collaboration, grit and teamwork to successfully execute projects. She also believes in the importance of peer recognition as an important building block in creating an excellent company culture. Jeneth is actively involved in NextGen Leaders, an employee resource group (ERG) which seeks to bring together diverse individuals across the generations to network, collaborate, and make meaningful connections.
